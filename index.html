Douglas Rossi Melo

Professor @ Univali · Researcher @ LEDS

Bachelor in Computer Engineering from the University of Vale do Itajaí (2008), Master in Applied Computer Science from the University of Vale do Itajaí (2012) and PhD in Electrical Engineering from the Federal University of Santa Catarina (2020). He is currently a Professor at the University of Vale do Itajaí and a Researcher at the Laboratory of Embedded and Distributed Systems. His topics of interest are Systems-on-Chip, Networks-on-Chip and Fault Tolerance.
